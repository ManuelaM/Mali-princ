The Little Prince

Antoine de Saint-Exupery

The Little Prince is not a novel, but a novella and the most famous work of a French author,
and the most translated book in the French language.

OVERVIEW

The Little Prince is a poetic tale whose narrator becomes pilot and one day his plane
crashes in Sahara dessert. It was written during a period when Antoine fled to North
America during the WWII. He once said that
The war is not an adventure, it is a disease. It is like typhus.
During his numerous adventures, he meets The Rose, a Snake, the Baobab etc. The story
ends with the narrator's drawing of the landscape where the prince and the narrator
met and where the snake took the prince's life.
